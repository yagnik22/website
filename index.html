Patient Information

Frequently Asked Questions

What conditions does Dr. Kumble treat? +
Dr. Kumble treats the full spectrum of heart rhythm disorders including atrial fibrillation, SVT, ventricular tachycardia, heart block, and inherited conditions such as Brugada syndrome and long QT syndrome.
What is a cardiac electrophysiologist? +
A cardiac electrophysiologist is a cardiologist with additional subspecialty training in the electrical system of the heart, diagnosing and treating arrhythmias using catheter ablation and implantable devices.
What is catheter ablation and is it safe? +
Catheter ablation is a minimally invasive procedure to eliminate the source of an abnormal rhythm. It is well-established with high success rates and a strong safety profile.
